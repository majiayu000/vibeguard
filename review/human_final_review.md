# Human Final Review

Human maintainers own final approval and merge readiness.

## Check

- The linked issue is valid and still desired.
- The spec, if required, was accepted.
- The diff matches the declared scope.
- Agent findings were addressed or explicitly rejected.
- CI is fresh for the current head.
- Security, permissions, and release impact are understood.
- Ownership approval is present when required.

## Decision

Use one:

- approve
- request_changes
- needs_spec
- needs_info
- close_not_planned
- reserved_internal
